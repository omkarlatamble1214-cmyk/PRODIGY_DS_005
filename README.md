# PRODIGY_DS_05

## Task 05: Traffic Accident Data Analysis

### Objective

Analyze traffic accident data to identify patterns related to road conditions, weather, and time of day. Visualize accident hotspots and contributing factors.

### Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Google Colab

### Analysis Performed

1. Accident analysis by weather condition
2. Accident analysis by road condition
3. Accident analysis by time of day

### Results

* Rainy and wet road conditions showed higher accident counts.
* Night-time accidents were more frequent than daytime accidents.
* Weather and road conditions significantly influenced accident occurrence.

### Files Included

* PRODIGY_DS_05.ipynb
* weather_analysis.png
* road_condition_analysis.png
* time_of_day_analysis.png
* README.md

### Conclusion

Traffic accidents are influenced by environmental conditions and time of day. Visual analysis helps identify risk factors and supports road safety planning.
